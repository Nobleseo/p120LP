Project 120 Overview

Project 120 Africa is a high-impact youth empowerment initiative focused on equipping the next generation with essential tech, media, and business skills. At the core of this project is the commitment to unlocking the full potentials of 120 skilled/unskilled yet ambitious individuals each project month, with the expertise, network and real world experience required to thrive and also offering the participants the knowledge, tools and practical exposure necessary to excel in today’s highly competitive global landscape.

Benefits

  • Hands-on training with industry experts
  • Certification and digital credentials
  • Networking and mentorship opportunities
  • Portfolio-ready projects and internships
  • Access to an exclusive alumni community
  • Personalized career coaching
  • Exposure to cutting-edge technologies
  • Opportunities for remote work
  • Entrepreneurship support
  • Global networking events

Project Structure

Registration

4-week sign-up per cohort with early bird pricing.

Conference

2 days intensive world class sessions with panels, workshops and pitchdecks.

Internship

8 weeks intensive internship hands on experience in chosen skill set accompanied with practical sessions and experiments.

Certification

Award ceremony recognizing all 120 participants, during this ceremony the project will doll out grants in cash and other prizes to outstanding participants.

Onboarding

After the internship all 120 participants will be given access to an already saturated market of high paying jobs and also availed a 3 months mentorship.
