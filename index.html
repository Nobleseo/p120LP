Program Overview

Project 120 Africa is a grassroots movement targeting 120 youth in each African country to receive digital skills training, mentorship, and entrepreneurial support. Our mission is to reduce unemployment and bridge the digital divide.

Benefits

  • Access to world-class digital skills training
  • 1-on-1 mentorship with industry experts
  • Opportunities for internships, jobs, and funding
  • Join a continent-wide alumni network

Program Structure

The program runs in 3 phases over 12 weeks: training (4 weeks), mentoring (4 weeks), and project incubation (4 weeks).

Skills You Will Learn

  • Web Development & Design
  • Graphics & Multimedia
  • Digital Marketing
  • Entrepreneurship & Leadership
